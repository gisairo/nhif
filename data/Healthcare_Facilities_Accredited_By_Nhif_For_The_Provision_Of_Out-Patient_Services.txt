HEALTHCARE FACILITIES ACCREDITED BY NHIF
FOR THE PROVISION OF OUT-PATIENT SERVICES
The National Hospital Insurance Fund (NHIF) endeavours to expand access of
benefits covered under in-patient and out-patient utilisation.

therefore advised to consider choice based on suitability to their health needs and
convenience of access to care.

Listed below are the NHIF accredited facilities that have capacity to serve the NHIF
beneficiaries on the enhanced cover to include out-patient care provision. The list
is progressive and shall be updated from time to time considering that a number
of facilities have expressed interest to participate in service provision.

Eligibility
•	 Members who are duly registered by NHIF and their declared dependants
•	 Members contributions  MUST be up-to-date

NHIF therefore wishes to inform its members and declared beneficiaries that the
opportunity to choose a preferred Out-patient service provider is open and valid
for a period between 15th to 30th June 2015.
